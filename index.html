Your Data, Yours
The Problem

Your health data is everywhere
except where you need it.

You're living longer than any generation before you. But the system tracking your health was designed for sick care, not preventative longevity.

Fragmented Records

Lab results in one portal, imaging in another, prescriptions in a third. Your primary care doctor doesn't see your cardiologist's notes.

Reactive, Not Preventative

Traditional medicine waits for something to break. By the time symptoms appear, years of preventable decline have already happened.

Wearable Data Goes Nowhere

Your Oura Ring tracks sleep, HRV, and resting heart rate around the clock — but that data never reaches your physician.

You Don't Own Your Data

Your most personal information lives on corporate servers you don't control. Switching providers means starting over.

The Solution

One platform. Complete picture.
Built for longevity.

Healthspan consolidates your entire health profile into a single, patient-owned platform — calibrated to longevity research, not population averages.

Trend Engine

14-day rolling averages compared against 90-day baselines across 15 health metrics. See where you're heading, not just where you are.

Medicine 3.0 Targets

Every target range is calibrated to longevity research from Peter Attia's Outlive framework — not generic population averages.

Course Corrections

Automatic alerts when metrics decline or drift outside target zones. Catch problems before they become diagnoses.

Overall Health Score

Weighted composite factoring zone status and trend direction across all domains. One number, deep context behind it.

Smart Device Integration

Oura Ring data flows in automatically. Fitbit, Apple Health, and Garmin integrations on the roadmap.

Open Source, Self-Hosted

Your health data stays on infrastructure you control. No vendor lock-in. No corporate data harvesting. Fork it, run it, own it.

Live Dashboard

Data you can actually act on.

Seven dashboard views across sleep, activity, heart, readiness, and resilience — all driven by real wearable data.
